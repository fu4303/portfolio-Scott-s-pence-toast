Giveaway issues

There's the rub

Becsue a lot of people will subscribe to newsletters with aliased email addresses (example:johndoe+newsletter@gmail.com) there's no real two way communication expected so the vast majority of people that sign up only around a third actually read emails from me.

I had budgeted five courses to give away (yes this is my own money) and only managed to giveaway three.

Apologies if you have received and email saying you've won something only to follow it up with "sorry it was time sensitive and I've withdrawn the offer".

I'll try to be clearer about communicating this in the future.
